Welcome

At Website.com, we believe everyone deserves to have a website to follow the best of football's news. Our news guarantees certified, honest and balanced information, which is the first weapon in the fight against all forms of infox: rumours, propaganda, manipulations, click traps, etc...

We're excited to present you the best news of the moment !

Our story

We are two students in our 4th year of engineering school at ESILV, majoring in finance and specialising in Fintech. And we wish to bring our skills in web development programming to offer you a practical and safe website. We wanted to share our passion for sport with you through a platform summarizing the news of European football !

Features and Services

We propose you a site of sports news, displaying the classification of the European clubs, the best news of the moment as well as a possible subscription to our newsletter in order to receive by e-mail all the complete and certified news on a monthly basis.
